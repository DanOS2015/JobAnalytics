CV Format Guidelines

Your curriculum vitae must be in the following format before you upload it. This is a simple example of the format. Your CV should contain 5 sections:

Personal Information

This is your personal information and should be displayed at the top of your CV. It must be the first section of you CV. It should contain the following information:

  1. Full name
  2. Current address
  3. Email
  4. Phone Number
Profile

This section should contain a very brief summary of your CV as well as what your objective is e.g. what kind of role you're looking for. This must be displayed after your personal section.

The next 3 sections can be displayed in any other in your CV, as long as they come after your profile section.

Education

This should list all the educational institutes you have attended. They must be specified in the following format:

(Years attended) (Name of Institute)
(Degree/Certificate/Qualification/Exam Results)
Work Experience

This should list all your previous work places and roles.They must be specified in the following format:

(Years attended) (Work Role), (Name of Employer)
(Description of your duties, tasks and skills your learnt)
Skills

This should list all your work skills. This section doesn't need to be in any particular format. However, make sure that you state your skills clearly.
